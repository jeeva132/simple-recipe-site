Paratha

Paratha is a popular Indian flatbread that's delicious and versatile. It can be stuffed with various fillings or enjoyed plain with a side of chutney, yogurt, or pickles. Learn how to make soft and flaky parathas at home with this easy recipe.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : paratha indian flatbread breakfast

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, salt, and water to make a soft and smooth dough. Knead the dough for a few minutes until it's elastic. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.

step 2

Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Roll each ball into a small circle using a rolling pin. Brush the circle with ghee or oil.

step 3

Fold the circle in half to form a semi-circle. Brush the semi-circle with ghee or oil again and fold it once more to create a triangle.

step 4

Roll out the triangle into a thin paratha, ensuring that it's evenly rolled.

step 5

Heat a griddle or flat pan over medium-high heat. Place the rolled paratha on the hot griddle and cook until it puffs up and turns golden brown on both sides, brushing with more ghee or oil as needed.

ingredients

2 cups whole wheat flour

Water as needed

Salt to taste

Ghee or oil for brushing

tools

Rolling Pin

Mixing Bowl

Griddle or Flat Pan

Brush for Oil/Ghee
